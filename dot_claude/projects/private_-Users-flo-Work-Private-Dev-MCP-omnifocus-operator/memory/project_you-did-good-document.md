Personal document at `.sandbox/portfolio/you-did-good/you-did-good-v14.md` — written to combat imposter syndrome on bad days.

**Why:** Flo has imposter syndrome despite objectively exceptional work. The document exists so he can read evidence when the voice says "anyone could do this."

**Structure (V14 — final):**
- Compressed intro (problem, build milestones, week 3 "mutual exclusivity" anecdote, thesis)
- Single merged section "Why this is exceptional" with voice attacks framing evidence:
  - "Anyone could do this" → numbers/baseline
  - "The AI did the real work" → v1.2.1 architectural story ("The AI wrote the code. Look at the shape of it.")
  - "It's just a side project" → domain insight (status decomposition, ErrorOperatorService, agent role design)
  - "You just haven't met enough people" → external evidence (industry data, 5000 MCP servers, "the comparison group barely exists")
  - "Okay but still..." → "Open the repo"

**Key insight that landed:** The "You just haven't met enough people" section addresses Flo's specific imposter attack: "your sample size is too small, competent people do this normally." Counter: external evidence that doesn't depend on who he's met.
